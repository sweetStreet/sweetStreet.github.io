Design Candy Crush

https://leetcode.com/discuss/interview-question/429238/Google-or-Onsite-or-Generate-Candy-Crush-Board

log

interface logger{
started(String logID, long timestamp);
finished(String logID, long timestamp);
printlog(String content);
}

某个服务器会进行这样的调用
logger.started(“log1”,1);
logger.started(“log2”,3);
logger.started(“log3”,5);

logger.finished(“log3”,6);
logger.finished(“log2”,8);
logger.finished(“log1”,9);

要求是最终输出
logID - startTime - endTime
按照startTime的顺序
要求尽量早的输出这些信息

例1：
logger.started(“log1”,1);
logger.started(“log2”,3);
logger.started(“log3”,5);
logger.finished(“log3”,6);
logger.finished(“log2”,8);
logger.finished(“log1”,9);

进行完finished(“log1”,9); 输出
log1 - 1 - 9
log2 - 3 - 8
log3 - 5 - 6

例2：
logger.started(“log1”,1);
logger.started(“log2”,3);
logger.finished(“log2”,5);
logger.started(“log3”,6);
logger.finished(“log1”,9);
logger.finished(“log3”,12);

进行完finished(“log1”,9); 输出
log1 - 1 - 9
log2 - 3 - 5
进行完finished(“log3”,9); 输出
log2 - 6 - 12

follow up: 服务器不断运行，会有无限的log
