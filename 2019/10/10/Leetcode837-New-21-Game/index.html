[Leetcode837]New_21_Game

Yu' Blog
2019-10-10
algorithm, dynamic programming, leetcode

Alice plays the following game, loosely based on the card game “21”.

Alice starts with 0 points, and draws numbers while she has less than K points. During each draw, she gains an integer number of points randomly from the range [1, W], where W is an integer. Each draw is independent and the outcomes have equal probabilities.

Alice stops drawing numbers when she gets K or more points. What is the probability that she has N or less points?

Example 1:

Input: N = 10, K = 1, W = 10
Output: 1.00000
Explanation:  Alice gets a single card, then stops.

Example 2:

Input: N = 6, K = 1, W = 10
Output: 0.60000
Explanation:  Alice gets a single card, then stops.
In 6 out of W = 10 possibilities, she is at or below N = 6 points.

Example 3:

Input: N = 21, K = 17, W = 10
Output: 0.73278

solution
这是一个和爬楼梯问题类似的动态规划问题，我们可以最终确定Alice得到的点数在[K, K+W-1]，恰好获得点数n的概率为dp[n] = 1/W(dp[n-w] +dp[n-w+1] + … + dp[n-1])
乘1/w的原因是获取任意一个点的概率都是1/W。由于题目规定了K这个值，也就是说在n>K+1的情况下，dp[n]是无法通过dp[n-1]得到的，因此dp[n] = 1/Wdp[n-w] + 1/Wdp[n-w+1] + … + 1/W*dp[K-1]。最后点数小于N的概率就是dp[N]+…+dp[K-1]
